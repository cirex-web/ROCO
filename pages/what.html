What?

RoCo is a club, supervised by Mr. Hanas, of students who are passionate about coding and the design of robots.

A comprehensive curriculum

In the club, lessons are provided on a variety subjects, such as Python, JS, Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and even Amazon Alexa!!! These lessons are for all levels, so don't discouraged just because you are new to coding.

Many coding opportunities

There are also several opportunities to participate in events where you can practice your skills. You can work on coding projects, participate in hackathons, and try out CTF.
